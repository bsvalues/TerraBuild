Security & Compliance

Containerization

AI Agent Swarm

Harness the power of specialized AI agents to optimize infrastructure cost assessments.

FactorTuner

Automatically fine-tunes cost factors based on historical data and regional economic indicators.

CurveTrainer

Develops specialized cost curves to accurately model non-linear relationships in building costs.

ScenarioAgent

Creates and analyzes what-if scenarios to predict cost outcomes under different conditions.

BOEArguer

Generates justifications and evidence for Board of Equalization hearings and appeals.

BenchmarkGuard

Monitors calculations for consistency and flags outliers that may need additional review.

Full Swarm Runner

Orchestrates all AI agents to work together, producing comprehensive assessments with maximum efficiency.
